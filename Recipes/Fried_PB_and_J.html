Fried Peanut Butter and Jelly

Description

A fried take on a savory, sweet classic.

Ingredients

  • 4 Slice White Bread
  • 1/4 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Jelly
  • 2 Cups Cooking Oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat Fryer to 375 degrees F
  2. Flatten bread with rolling pen. Spread half the slice of bread with peanut butter, the other half with jelly.
  3. Fold in half and crimp the edges.
  4. Fry for around 1-2 minutes, until golden brown.
  5. Cool for 5 minutes before serving. Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
