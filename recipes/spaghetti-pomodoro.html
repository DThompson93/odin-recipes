Spaghetti Pomodoro

Description

Spaghetti Pomodoro is a simple pasta dish with a delicious tomato sauce, fresh basil and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Spaghetti
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Soften the Onions in a pan with Olive Oil.
  2. Add the Tomatoes and season with Salt.
  3. Once the Tomatoes have softened and created a sauce, add the Basil.
  4. Bring a pan of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti until al dente, according to package instructions. Leave 1/2 cup of the pasta water and then drain the pasta.
  5. Mix the pasta and the tomato sauce together, add in the cup of hot pasta water and stir
  6. Plate up and garnish with basil and parmesan cheese.
