Garlic Mushrooms

Description

Garlic Mushrooms are simply great serves as a side dish, on toast or in pasta.

Ingredients

  • 40g Butter
  • 500g White Mushrooms
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • Large handful of parsley leaves

Steps

  1. Cook the Mushrooms in a frying pan with butter
  2. With the mushrooms glistening and cooked through, toss through the garlic.
  3. Stir in most of the parsley
  4. Serve as a side dish, on toast or tossed through spaghetti, with a sprinkling of the remaining parsley.
